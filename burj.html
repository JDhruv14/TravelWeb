The Great

Burj Khalifa



  • Day 1: Arrival and Downtown Dubai
    - Morning: Arrive in Dubai, transfer to hotel, check-in.
    - Afternoon: Explore Downtown Dubai.
    - Evening: Visit The Dubai Mall and have dinner.

  • Day 2: Burj Khalifa and Downtown Dubai
    - Morning: Visit Burj Khalifa (At the Top observation decks).
    - Afternoon: Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, lunch at The Dubai Mall.
    - Evening: Watch Dubai Fountain show, dinner with a view.

  • Day 3: Cultural and Historical Dubai
    - Morning: Visit Dubai Museum and Al Bastakiya Quarter.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at a traditional Emirati restaurant.
    - Evening: Abra ride on Dubai Creek, dinner by the creek.

  • Day 4: Modern Attractions
    - Morning: Mall of the Emirates, visit Ski Dubai.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at Mall of the Emirates, explore Dubai Marina.
    -Evening: Dinner cruise in Dubai Marina.

  • Day 5: Desert Safari
    - Morning: Relax at the hotel.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at the hotel or nearby, depart for desert safari.
    - Evening: Desert safari activities and BBQ dinner with entertainment.

  • Day 6: Abu Dhabi Day Trip
    - Morning: Early breakfast, visit Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.
    - Afternoon: Lunch in Abu Dhabi, visit Louvre Abu Dhabi.
    - Evening: Return to Dubai, dinner at the hotel or nearby.

  • Day 7: Adventure and Fun
    - Morning: Visit Aquaventure Waterpark at Atlantis The Palm.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at the waterpark, continue enjoying the park.
    - Evening: Dinner at a restaurant in Atlantis The Palm.

  • Day 8: Palm Jumeirah and Beach Day
    - Morning: Explore Palm Jumeirah, visit The Pointe.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at a beachside restaurant.
    - Evening: Relax at Jumeirah Beach, dinner nearby.

  • Day 9: Shopping and Souks
    - Morning: Visit Gold Souk and Spice Souk.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at a local restaurant, explore Dubai Frame.
    - Evening: Dinner at a restaurant with traditional ambiance.

  • Day 10: Leisure and Departure
    - Morning: Relax, final shopping or revisit favorite spots.
    - Afternoon: Lunch at the hotel, pack and prepare for departure.
    - Evening: Transfer to airport, depart Dubai.

Our mission at JetSetGo is to provide you with unforgettable experiences and hassle-free journeys. From exotic destinations to hidden gems, we curate trips that inspire and invigorate.
